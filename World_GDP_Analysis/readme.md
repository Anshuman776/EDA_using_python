## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes **World Gross Domestic Product (GDP)** data to understand:

- 🌎 Global economic growth trends  
- 🏳️ Individual country GDP performance  
- 📈 Comparison between World GDP and specific country GDP  
- 📉 Economic growth patterns over time  

The analysis is divided into **two main datasets**:

1. Individual Country GDP Dataset  
2. World GDP vs Individual Country Comparison Dataset  

---

## 🗂 Dataset Description

### 1️⃣ Individual Country GDP Dataset

This dataset contains:

- Country Name  
- Year  
- GDP Value (Current or Constant USD)  
- Annual Growth Rate (if available)  

### 📌 Purpose

- Analyze economic growth of a specific country  
- Identify upward or downward trends  
- Detect recession and expansion periods  
- Calculate annual growth rates  

---

### 2️⃣ World GDP vs Individual Country Dataset

This dataset includes:

- Year  
- Total World GDP  
- Selected Country GDP  
- GDP Share Percentage  

### 📌 Purpose

- Compare a country's contribution to global GDP  
- Measure economic dominance  
- Analyze long-term comparative growth  
- Benchmark country performance against global economy  

---

## 📐 Key Economic Formula Used

### GDP Growth Rate Formula

\[
GDP\ Growth\ Rate = \frac{GDP_t - GDP_{t-1}}{GDP_{t-1}} \times 100
\]

Where:
- \( GDP_t \) = Current Year GDP  
- \( GDP_{t-1} \) = Previous Year GDP
